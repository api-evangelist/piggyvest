This guide explores the FIRE movement framework adapted for the Nigerian context, addressing challenges like high inflation, currency devaluation, and extended family financial obligations. It introduces a "Naija FIRE plan" using a 2.5% withdrawal rate instead of the standard 4% rule, accounting for high-inflation economies. The post provides practical steps toward financial independence through disciplined savings, investment in naira and dollar assets, and building passive income streams.
